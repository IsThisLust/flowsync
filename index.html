The Productivity Crisis

Indian Professionals Lose 2–3 Hours Every Day

Scattered priorities, back-to-back meetings, constant notifications — your best hours vanish before you even start deep work.

Task Overload

67% of professionals say they can't distinguish urgent from important. Without clear priorities, everything feels equally pressing — and nothing gets done well.

Constant Distractions

The average knowledge worker gets interrupted every 11 minutes. Recovering deep focus takes 23 minutes — that's hours of lost cognitive energy every single day.

Burnout & Imbalance

Without boundaries, work bleeds into evenings and weekends. 78% of Indian employees report work-life imbalance, leading to chronic stress and declining performance.

That's ₹45,000+ per employee lost every month in wasted productive hours.

AI-Powered Features

Your Personal AI Productivity Co-pilot

FlowSync doesn't just organize your to-do list — it learns your work patterns, protects your peak hours, and coaches you toward sustainable high performance.

AI Task Prioritization

Combines the Eisenhower Matrix with your personal work history to auto-rank tasks. No more guessing what to tackle first — FlowSync knows your patterns.

Smart Daily Schedule

AI builds your ideal day — blocking focus time during your peak hours, batching meetings, and leaving room for breaks. Syncs seamlessly with Google Calendar.

Focus Timer & DND Mode

Pomodoro-based focus sessions with adaptive intervals. Automatically enables DND and tracks distractions so you can protect your deep-work windows.

Work-Life Balance Score

A real-time score (0–100) that tracks overtime, break frequency, and evening screen time. Get nudges before burnout hits — because productivity without health is unsustainable.

Weekly AI Reports

Every Sunday, get a personalized performance report with trend analysis, habit insights, and actionable recommendations — written in plain language, not raw stats.

Built for India

Festival-aware scheduling, IST timezone intelligence, UPI-ready payments, and work patterns calibrated for Indian corporate culture. Not another Silicon Valley clone.
